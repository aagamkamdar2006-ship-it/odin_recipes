Veg Pizza

Description

Veg Pizza combines a crispy homemade crust with savory tomato sauce and a colorful blast of fresh vegetables like onions, peppers, tomatoes, and mushrooms—all topped with golden, melted mozzarella cheese.

This delicious plant-based pizza is easy to customize with your favorite toppings and herbs. Great for lunches, dinners, or parties, it brings together the taste and textures of Italian cooking in a vibrant, vegetarian form.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup warm water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp dry yeast
  • 2 cups maida (all-purpose flour)
  • Salt
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 tbsp pizza sauce
  • Sliced mushrooms, onions, capsicum, jalapeños, black olives
  • ½ cup mozzarella cheese, grated
  • Mixed herbs/oregano

Steps

  1. Mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it activate for 5 minutes.
  2. Add flour, salt, olive oil; knead into a soft dough. Let it rise for 2 hours.
  3. Punch dough, shape onto pizza tray, spread sauce.
  4. Arrange veggies and cheese; sprinkle herbs.
  5. Bake at 250°C for 12-15 minutes until golden.
  6. Slice and serve hot.
